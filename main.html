Interstellar
Actors Quotes

'Interstellar' is a 2014 sci-fi film directed by Christopher Nolan.

The film is not only a treat to the eyes of the audience. It also a story that will make you invest in it and think of human actions and their consequences.

True to the Nolan spirit, 'Interstellar' embodies in itself grave storytelling, playing with time, wit, and a star cast of Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, MacKenzie Foy, Jessica Chastain, and many more. The story of the film revolves around the future possibility of the earth being inhabitable. In a last attempt to save the last traces of humankind, NASA, decides to send a crew headed by McConaughey's to look for a planet that might sustain human life. Here is a collection of quotes from the popular movie for you to enjoy.
